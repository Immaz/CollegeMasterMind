Types of Students We Work with Often!



  • High Achiever

This type of student is at the top of their academic and achievement game





We often see these types with unweighted 3.9+ GPAs, tons of extracurriculars, taking the hardest classes at their school and who might have high test scores.

  • Easygoing

This type of student is typically at the top of their social or leisure game.





We often see these types with unweighted 3.0-3.8 GPAs, social or creative-focused extracurriculars and who might struggle with consistent motivation.

  • First Generation

This type of student was raised by parents born outside of the United States





First Generation students can either be easygoing or high achievers, but often the parents feel inexperienced in admissions and want guidance during the process.
